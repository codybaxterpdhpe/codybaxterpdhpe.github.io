What is Discrimination?

Discrimination is the unjust treatment of an individual based on one of their physical attributes. It is commonly based on age, race and gender.

How Does it Impact it's Victims?

Discrimination is very harmful to peoples mental health, making people feel as if they aren't good enough. In some extreme cases, continuous discrimination has led to suicide. So please, treat everyone fairly!
